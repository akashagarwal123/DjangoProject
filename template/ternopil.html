About Ternopil National Medical University, Ukraine

Ternopil National Medical University is ranked among one of the top universities in Ukraine. It was established on September 1, 1957. The university has got the third rank with IVth highest level of accreditation by Ministry of Health and Education in Ukraine. There are 800 foreign students from 48 countries all over the world. Ternopil National Medical University is well-known for cultural traditions. Around 10,000 students are studying medical course at the university. The Ternopil Medical University has 30 departments in which 148 teachers are working. Today, the university has become a scientific, medical, pedagogical, cultural & educational center of Ukraine. In the year 2004, the specialized council in Paediatrics, Obstetrics & Gynaecology was established.

Eligibility Criteria

For studying medicine at V.N. Karazin KNU, a candidate needs to qualify the following criteria:


Students have to submit legal and authorized documents

Students must have passed 10+2 with an aggregate of 55% in PCB.

It is mandatory to have original certificates of respective documents.

For Indian students, they have to submit a copy of a nationality certificate.


Admission Procedure for Students


  • Step 1

    The candidates who wish to apply for MBBS at TNMU do forward your resume to CLICK HERE that is approved by the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine. It usually takes 10-15 days to review the applications.

  • Step 2

    Eligible candidate will get an invitation letter from Ternopil National Medical University either by mail or by courier.

  • Step 3

    The candidate should then submit an invitation letter to the Embassy of Ukraine for applying for a student visa.

  • Step 4

    After getting a student visa, students must report to the International Students Office of Ternopil National Medical University.

  • Step 5

    By confirming the arrival time, students should inform to the International Students Office for the further application process.

  • Step 6

    The admission Process will be completed when the candidate signs an agreement with the university and pay the tuition fee for the 1st year.
