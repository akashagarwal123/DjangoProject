Faculties at the Poltava State Medical University


Faculty of Medicine

Medicine studies at Poltava Medical Academy is conducted exactly according to international standard of medicine study. Medicine study in Poltava is modernized with highly professional teaching staff and advanced curriculum.

Faculty of Dentistry

Dentistry faculty at Poltava university has skilled teaching faculty and advanced laboratories. Dentistry department of the university is one of the best department of dentistry in Europe.

Faculty of Pediatrics

At the paediatric faculty of Poltava University students master knowledge at many chairs during 6 years of study, where they study anatomy, pathology, physiology, and hygiene of childish age.


Faculty of Nursing

The duration of Nursing course is 2 years for full time and 3 years for part time. The graduates can occupy administrative posts and work as medical nurses of different profile.

Faculty of Postgraduate Education

The main objective of the postgraduate training is to allow general practitioners to improve their skills for further certification and the students of medical institutions to continue their education.

Faculty of Pharmacy

It is a five year course awarding the “Bachelor of Pharmacy” or “Pharmacist” degree to students. The prime departments are: pharmacology, in-organic chemistry, biology, analytical, physical and organic chemistry.
